# Calgary Pro Plumbing Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

The Calgary Pro Plumbing Business Intelligence Dashboard is an end-to-end data analytics project developed to demonstrate how business intelligence can improve decision-making for a residential and commercial plumbing company.

This project follows the complete analytics lifecycle, from understanding business requirements and preparing data to SQL analysis, dashboard development in Power BI, and actionable business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Many small businesses rely on spreadsheets and manual reporting, making it difficult to quickly understand business performance and identify trends.

The objective of this project was to transform operational data into meaningful insights that help management monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify opportunities for improvement, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze business performance using operational data.
- Identify revenue and service trends.
- Evaluate technician productivity.
- Monitor customer satisfaction.
- Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide business recommendations based on analytical findings.
